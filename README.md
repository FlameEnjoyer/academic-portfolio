# Roby Pratama Sitepu - Mechanical Engineering | Combustion & Energy Research Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio repository! I am a fast-track Bachelor and Master's Mechanical Engineering student at the Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) specializing in sustainable energy research. My work bridges the gap between physical experiments and computational modeling, with a strong focus on the combustion of non-carbon fuels like ammonia and hydrogen. This repository serves as a centralized hub for my academic and professional engineering milestones.


## 🔬 Core Competencies & Skills

- **Experimental Combustion:** Flame stability analysis, emissions profiling (NOx, CO), optical diagnostics, and burner design.
- **Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD):** OpenFOAM (C++), Ansys Fluent, FVM, turbulence modeling, and multiphase reacting flows.
- **Process & Thermodynamic Simulation:** Aspen HYSYS, Aspen Plus, DWSIM, power plant cycle optimization, and Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) integration.
- **Programming & AI/ML:** Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn), C++, MATLAB, Physics-Informed Machine Learning (PIML), Surrogate Modeling, and Optimization Algorithms (Genetic Algorithms, Simulated Annealing).

## 🚀 Highlighted Projects

### Advanced Combustion Research
- **Optimization of Hydrogen m-GT Combustor:** Master's thesis research utilizing OpenFOAM and surrogate models to optimize combustor geometry and operating conditions, targeting lower emissions and higher thermal efficiency.
- **Partially Cracked Ammonia Combustion:** Bachelor's thesis and ongoing collaborative research with the University of Tokyo investigating flame stability, shape, and NOx emission characteristics of NH3-H2-N2 flames in co-flow and swirling burners.

### Computational Solvers & Software Engineering
- **Viscous-Inviscid Interaction Solver:** Developed a custom Python aerodynamic analysis tool coupling the Panel Method with Thwaites' Method, validated against JavaFoil data.
- **Inviscid Burgers Equation FVM Solver:** Engineered a finite volume method solver in Python with rigorous flux-limiter schemes to accurately capture shock wave formation.
- **Riser Design Web Application:** Designed and deployed a Python/Streamlit web app for calculating offshore riser wall thicknesses and visualizing safety factors in compliance with API and ASME standards.

### Energy Systems & Process Simulation
- **Geothermal Recuperative ORC System:** Modeled and simulated a recuperative organic Rankine cycle binary system integrated with a double flasher geothermal plant using Aspen HYSYS to quantify efficiency gains.
- **Solar PV Technoeconomic Design:** Designed an optimized, cost-effective Solar PV system in Kertosari Village to power agricultural machinery, conducting extensive LCOE and ROI feasibility analyses.

### Hardware & Mechanical Design
- **ORCA Box-Wing UAV:** Contributed to the design and fabrication of a tiltrotor UAV for disaster management, which secured 2nd place at the Indonesian National Flying Robot Competition 2023.
- **Sustainable Agricultural Tech:** Engineered and manufactured functional prototype machines, including an automatic cinnamon peeler that boosts productivity by 6x and a solar-powered chili dryer that accelerates drying times up to 10x.
